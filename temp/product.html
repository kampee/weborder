Is a northerners favorite dish which the tourists like to buy as a gift for their friends and relatives. It is typically hot and has a spice smell. People usual dip many kinds of vegetables into it or mix it in a dish of hot steamed rice. The uniqueness of our product is that we select fresh and clean ingredients. The procedures are supervised by our nutrition experts. Our Nun Prik Noom is preservative - free; but sterilized for the best instead. There are 4 Kinds of Num Prik Noom; Original flavor, Pla-Ra(fermented fish), Mang Da and mild - hot original..
